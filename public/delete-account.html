Account Deletion Process

Option 1: Delete Within the App

The easiest way to delete your account is directly through the Style Chat AI app:

  1. Open the Style Chat AI app on your device
  2. Go to Settings or Profile section
  3. Tap on "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your decision
  5. Your account will be permanently deleted immediately

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you're unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by email:

  1. Send an email from the email address associated with your account
  2. Include your username or user ID if known
  3. State that you want to delete your account
  4. We'll process your request within 48 hours
Send Deletion Request

⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, chat history, preferences, and saved items will be permanently deleted.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your profile information and account credentials
  • All chat conversations and history
  • Saved preferences and settings
  • Style recommendations and favorites
  • Any uploaded photos or images
  • Usage data and analytics tied to your account

Data Retention: Some anonymized usage statistics may be retained for service improvement purposes, but they will not be linked to your personal information.
